Our game, Business Frenzy

Business Frenzy is a fun and exciting game based on buying and selling buildings

Players must buy buildings and sell buildings for money, zones on the board dictate the prices of buildings and your salary.

  1. Green zone is the cheapest zone for buying buildings and your salary.
  2. Yellow zone is more expensive after the green zone, this affects the buying/selling and salary
  3. Orange zone is the second most expensive zone in the game, the risks and the rewards high here
  4. Red zone is the most expensive zone in the game. The rewards and the risks are very high here

Salaries in-game

In the green zone the salary(excluding the money from the buildings) is low at £10 per three turns, the yellow zone is slighty higher at £50 , the orange zone is high at £100 pounds per three turns. The red zone is very high at £150 per three turns


Buying and selling buildings

Building prices depend on the zone you are in, in each zone the price goes up by £15, and the salary from the buildings also go up by £20 in each zone

Every turn, you can buy buildings(prices above ^), sell them for building price in that zone minus £5

Also, every turn, you can buy extensions for buildings. Extensions cost £5 and they add £5 to the salary of the building
